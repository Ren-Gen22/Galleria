valentine's day Mascara

Valentine's day mascara is an artwork by Banksy that appeared on the side of a house in Margate, Kent, on 14 February, Valentine's Day, 2023. The work depicts a woman shutting a man in a refrigerator.

The piece is painted on the side of a house on Park Place, to the rear of Grosvenor Place in Margate. It depicts an injured woman with a missing tooth and 'swollen eye' dressed as a housewife from the 1950s shutting a man in a freezer.She is dressed in a blue apron with yellow washing up gloves. The work incorporates a refrigerator that had been abandoned in the street next to the house. Thanet District Council removed the fridge after the appearance of the artwork, claiming the "fridge freezer which is believed to have been part of the installation has been removed by council operatives on the grounds of safety as it was on public land", and would be returned once it had been 'made safe'.Other items by the piece were removed including an empty beer bottle, a broken garden chair and a crate.

Melody of the night

Melody of Midnight is an abstract oil painting by Leonid Afremov.Afremov Is a modern impressionist painter who uses a pallet knife and oil paints to paint his texturized, vibrant pictures of landscapes.

A piece of art like “Melody at Midnight” is a representation to me of “place”, an abstract meaning in this world. “Place” becomes a place when it takes on meaning because of our experience with a particular place or thing in my interpretation (Neumann and Price). According to the article “Defining Place” by Tim Creswell, “place is not just a thing in the world but a way of understanding the world”. Art is part of our culture and part of our earth writing. Art gives us as a human experience.

by Leonid Afremov (45)

Dreams at sunset

Dreams at sunset was created by artist Olha Vyacheslavovna Darchuk in 2020. This art piece is a Paintings artwork. The style of this artwork is best described as Abstract, Impressionism. The genre portrayed in this piece of art is Analytical art, Landscape. The artwork was created in Canvas, Oil, Painting. The size of the original art is 60 (cms) H x 120 (cms) W.

Words which artist Olha Vyacheslavovna Darchuk feels best describe this work of art are: Sunset art, dream art, colorful art, landscape art, abstract painting, sky art, landscape bright, sunset colorful, for interior, wall decor, landscape, multicolor art, seasons.
